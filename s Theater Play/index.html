A Story Told Beyond Words

Set across a single unforgettable night, The Last Curtain follows a fading theatre company confronting the choices that shaped their careers. As memory and performance blur together, the company must decide what story is worth telling one final time. Staged with a live ensemble and a spare, intentional set, the production leans on presence over spectacle.

  • Original Production

    A new script developed over two years, written specifically for the Grand Hall Theatre stage.

  • Live Ensemble

    Performed live by a resident ensemble cast with no recorded audio or pre-staged effects.

  • Limited Season

    A short, defined run of performances before the production closes permanently.
